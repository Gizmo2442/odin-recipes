Tuna Casserole Pie

Description

The Tuna Casserole Pie is a unique take on the classic casserole dish. It features a delicious combination of tuna fish, grape jelly, and spinach that creates a sweet and savory flavor that is sure to tantalize your taste buds. The pie crust provides a perfect base for the filling, while the whipped cream and marshmallow mixture on top add a creamy and fluffy texture. With a golden brown crust and melted marshmallows, this casserole pie is a true feast for the senses.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna fish
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 box of frozen spinach
  • 1 can of whipped cream
  • 1 bag of marshmallows
  • 1 pie crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the tuna fish, grape jelly, and frozen spinach. Mix well until the tuna is coated with the mixture.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the whipped cream and marshmallows together.
  4. Place the pie crust in a casserole dish and pour the tuna mixture into it.
  5. Pour the whipped cream and marshmallow mixture on top of the tuna mixture.
  6. Bake the casserole in the oven for 35-40 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the marshmallows are melted.
